Lagos State operates under a democratically elected government that includes the executive, legislature and judiciary. The Governor leads the executive arm, supported by commissioners and state ministries such as the Ministry of Transportation, Ministry of Education and Ministry of Health. The Lagos State House of Assembly is responsible for lawmaking and oversight. Key government agencies include LAMATA for transportation planning, LASRRA for citizen registration, LAWMA for waste management, LASEMA for emergency response and the Lagos Internal Revenue Service for tax administration.
