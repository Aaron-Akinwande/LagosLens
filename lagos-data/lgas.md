Lagos State is divided into twenty Local Government Areas. These include Agege, Ajeromi-Ifelodun, Alimosho, Amuwo-Odofin, Apapa, Badagry, Epe, Eti-Osa, Ibeju-Lekki, Ifako-Ijaiye, Ikeja, Ikorodu, Kosofe, Lagos Island, Lagos Mainland, Mushin, Ojo, Oshodi-Isolo, Shomolu and Surulere. Ikeja serves as the capital of Lagos State. The LGAs collectively support the administrative, political and service delivery structure of the state.
