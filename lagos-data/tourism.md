Lagos is a major tourism destination with a mix of beaches, cultural sites and urban attractions. Popular beaches include Tarkwa Bay, Elegushi Beach and Landmark Beach. Historic attractions include Freedom Park, Nike Art Gallery and the National Museum Lagos. The Lekki Conservation Centre features a canopy walkway and wildlife viewing. Markets such as Balogun Market and Lekki Arts and Crafts Market draw visitors seeking shopping experiences. The city also hosts numerous entertainment events, concerts and nightlife venues.
